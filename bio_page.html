About



Welcome to my portfolio and thank you for visiting! My professional background has been rooted in the green industry and I am experienced in arborculture, fine gardening, landscape design and estate management. As I have worked within corporate and start-up sized companies alike, I am comfortable interfacing with groups and business scenarios of any size and theme.

My current focus is garden design and estate consulting as well as social media development and management. I love to make people happy by illuminating their visions, passions and goals in tangible ways. Additionally I am studying to become a full-stack web developer and look forward to contributing my creativity and enthusiasm for business growth to the digital world as I move forward.





When I'm not working, I love to travel with my fiance Alec and out cat Bailey. We have explored much of the Mediterranean and European Alps, which has hugely transformed my appreciation for global diversity and the unique ways different cultures blend their history with modern amenities. We also regularly drive across our own country to visit friends and family as well as sooth our adventureous spirits.

In addition to traveling, I love trail running, kayaking, painting, reading and learning.
